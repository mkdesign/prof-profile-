      my complete biography !

      in order to get more familiar with me,you can read my biography.

      Currently I work in Bioelectromagnetics (BEMG) research group at Iran University of Science and Technology (IUST) as a research assistant. I will start working on my master thesis by September 2012 in Computational Electromagnetics Lab, under supervision of Dr. Amir Ahmad Shishegar.
      Mainly I work on the biological effects of Electromagnetic waves on human body.
